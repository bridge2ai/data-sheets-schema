AI-READI Flagship Dataset of Type 2 Diabetes
AI-READI Flagship Dataset of Type 2 Diabetes
The AI-READI dataset consists of multimodal data collected from individuals with and without Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (T2DM) and harmonized across three data collection sites. Designed for future AI/ML studies, recruitment aimed to achieve approximately equal distribution of participants across diabetes severity. A standardized data acquisition protocol spans multiple domains (surveys, physical measurements, clinical data, imaging, wearable devices, ECG, laboratory results, blood glucose, and environmental variables such as home air quality) to enable downstream AI/ML analyses that are not feasible with claims or electronic health records alone. Some non-sensitive data are publicly downloadable under a license agreement; the full dataset, including sensitive elements, is available under a data use agreement (controlled access). As enrollment is ongoing, pilot release(s) and periodic updates may not be balanced across groups.
