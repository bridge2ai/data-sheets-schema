Collection Process

How was the data acquired?

https://chorus4ai.org/
CHoRUS
Patient-Focused Collaborative Hospital Repository Uniting Standards (CHoRUS) for Equitable AI
The CHoRUS (Collaborative Hospital Repository Uniting Standards) for Equitable AI is a flagship Bridge2AI dataset designed to improve recovery from acute illness through the creation of the most diverse, high-resolution, ethically sourced, AI-ready clinical dataset. This multicenter collaboration spans 20 academic centers (14 as data acquisition centers) and harmonizes multi-modal Electronic Health Record (EHR) data, physiological waveforms, clinical imaging, and text data using unified international standards (OMOP, DICOM, WFDB, EDF+). As of November 2024, the dataset covers 14 different hospitals with 23,400 unique patient admissions. The project emphasizes patient-focused ethical and legal approaches to manage privacy and bias while accounting for Social Determinants of Health. A visualization and annotation environment enables data labeling with clinically important prediction targets. Federated access mechanisms ensure balanced and diverse cohort representation. The dataset is available under controlled access with licensing agreements required for all users. CHoRUS collaborates with the Bridge2AI program and three other data generation projects to advance equitable AI in clinical care.
