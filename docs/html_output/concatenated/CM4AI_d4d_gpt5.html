Cell Maps for Artificial Intelligence - March 2025 Data Release (Beta)
Cell Maps for Artificial Intelligence - March 2025 Data Release (Beta)
March 2025 Beta data release of Cell Maps for Artificial Intelligence (CM4AI; CM4AI.org), a Functional Genomics Grand Challenge in the NIH Bridge2AI program. This release includes: - CRISPR perturb-seq in undifferentiated KOLF2.1J iPSCs - SEC-MS in undifferentiated KOLF2.1J iPSCs and iPSC-derived NPCs, neurons, and cardiomyocytes - Immunofluorescence (IF) subcellular images in MDA-MB-468 breast cancer cells under untreated conditions and chemotherapy perturbations (vorinostat and paclitaxel) Data are packaged as AI-ready RO-Crate datasets with provenance graphs and rich metadata using the FAIRSCAPE framework and will be augmented regularly through the end of the project. CM4AI is a collaboration among UCSD, UCSF, Stanford, UVA, Yale, University of Alabama at Birmingham, Simon Fraser University, and the Hastings Center.
